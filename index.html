Dentistry that stands the test of time

At Burba Dental Partners, our passion is beautiful, durable dentistry, from smile makeovers and complex restorative work to general dental care.

Drs. Stanley and Randall Burba

We've been delivering quality dental care for two generations. We know how to do dentistry that lasts.

A graduate of Georgetown University School of Dentistry, Dr. Stanley Burba opened Burba Dental Partners in 1968, where he now practices with his son, Dr. Randall Burba.

Dr. Randy is an accredited cosmetic dentist. While many dentists perform cosmetic services, fewer than one in 500 is actually accredited. He devotes over 100 hours a year to continuing education to stay up-to-date in all phases of dentistry—TMJ techniques, implant procedures, cosmetic and restorative dentistry.

Dr. Stanley is a Fellow of the International College of Dentists, and Dr. Randy is Past President of the New England Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry.

Our Services

We want you to have a smile that looks as good as it feels.

General dentistry

We provide dental care for every member of the family. We see patients of all ages, from routine check-ups to complex procedures, including crowns, bridges, and implants.

Dental implants

A highly trained dentist can provide implants that look and feel like your natural teeth. Dr. Randy has advanced training from the Zimmer Dental Implants Institute in Carlsbad, CA.

Smile makeovers

With every smile design, we provide a set of temporary provisionals for you to try. Dr. Randy is so convinced you’ll love your new smile that he offers a beautiful smile guarantee.

Invisalign

No longer do you need bulky, uncomfortable metal brackets filling your mouth in order to straighten your teeth.
